Burton Baton

Style Imperial Ale
Glass Snifter
Food Pairings Barbecue, Pepper Jack Cheese, Grilled game
Tasting Notes

A vanilla and oak sweetness hits you right off the bat. The backend hits you with some malt, then fades into a slightly bitter finish from the hops. Hop bitterness blend seamlessly with the malt and oak flavor.
