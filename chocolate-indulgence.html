Chocolate Indulgence Stout

Style Stout
Glass Snifter
Food Pairings Chocolate, Cheese, Beef.
Tasting Notes There is a lot of chocolate in the flavor of this beer, both the presence of a milk chocolate along with dry cocoa powder. Additionally there is some light plum sweetness and a small presence of alcohol as the beer warms.
