Flower Power IPA

Style Indian Pale Ale
Glass Pint
Food Pairings Grilled poultry, Shellfish, Salmon.
Tasting Notes

The floral hops deliver delicious floral, citrus, and pine flavors, bittersweet and very bright. By the end of each sip a lingering bitterness takes over the palette. Really crisp, refreshing and flavorful.
