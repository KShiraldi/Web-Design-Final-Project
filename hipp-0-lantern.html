Hipp-O-Lantern

Style Pumpkin Ale
Glass Tulip
Food Pairings Cranberry Sauce, Stuffing, Squash.
Tasting Notes

The flavor is strongly spiced by cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger. After the spice, the meat of the pumpkin is noticeable. A taste of honey with an alcoholic warmth lingers. It is brewed with real pumpkin meat and molasses.
