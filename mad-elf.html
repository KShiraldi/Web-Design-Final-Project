Mad Elf

Style Strong Dark Ale
Glass Chalice
Food Pairings Fruitcake, Muffins, Biscuits.
Tasting Notes Strong cherry notes and dark fruit come through with plenty of malt sweetness as the backbone. As the beer warms, flashes of brown sugar, honey, and alcohol come through.
